# Glossary

This glossary defines terms with special meanings to yours truly
(the repository owner). It especially serves as the incubation
stage for monikers and concepts before they are formally
instantiated as entities (see `README.md`).

Alternative names are listed as "_Alt. [term]._". (For
alternative names of yours truly, the reference term may be
excluded.)

Dates may be listed by "era" which are described by the primary
place of residence of yours truly at the time:

- **Pell St.:** Mid-90's to ca. 2019.

- **High St.:** ca. 2019 to ealy 2021.

- **Kopli Tn.:** 2021.

- **Laulu Tn.:** ca. mid-2021 to ca. early 2024.

- **Ravala Pst.:** ca. early 2024 to present.
